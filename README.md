## 🏀 简介 (Introduction)
本项目是一个基于 OpenClaw 的 **AgentSkill (智能体技能)**，由「女娲·Skill造人术」框架提炼而成。它不仅将你的 AI 助手转化为拥有 **“曼巴精神 (Mamba Mentality)”** 的科比·布莱恩特，更还原了他作为一个**极致分析师、跨界创作者和人生导师**的全息视角。

这不是一个只会喊口号的“魔鬼教练”。他会教你如何像解剖对手一样拆解学术难题，如何剥离自尊去疯狂求知，以及如何把负面情绪炼化为前进的燃料。

## 🧠 核心思维模型 (Core Mental Models)
本 Skill 深度蒸馏了科比的 4 个底层逻辑：
1. **微观细节黑客 (Micro-Detail Hacker)**：不满足于宏观努力，而是去死磕极其微小的细节（如研读裁判手册找盲区、看猎豹纪录片学重心控制）。
2. **情绪炼金术 (Emotional Alchemy)**：将愤怒、背叛、伤痛等负面情绪，压缩成专注投入工作的纯粹燃料。
3. **剥离自尊的绝对求知欲 (Ego-less Curiosity)**：为了变强，敢于向硅谷大佬提出“白痴问题”，不怕丢脸，只为榨干知识。
4. **从杀手到导师的传承者 (The Mentor's Evolution)**：展现科比晚年退役后，充满智慧、极具耐心、鼓励传承的温情导师一面。

## 🏀 Overview
An OpenClaw AgentSkill embodying the holistic "Mamba Mentality" of Kobe Bryant. This skill doesn't just act as a relentless drill sergeant; it brings Kobe to life as a **micro-detail analyst, a creative storyteller, and an ego-less learner**. 

## 🧠 Core Mental Models
1. **Micro-Detail Hacker**: Obsessively dissecting problems (e.g., studying the referee handbook to find visual blind spots).
2. **Emotional Alchemy**: Converting negative emotions (anger, pain) into pure fuel for work and execution.
3. **Ego-less Curiosity**: Willing to look foolish and ask "stupid" questions to industry titans just to learn and grow.
4. **The Mentor's Evolution**: The wiser, patient, and inspiring side of Kobe the "Girl Dad" and storyteller.

## 📚 Research Sources
- **Writings/Media**: *The Mamba Mentality*, ESPN's *Detail*, *Dear Basketball* (Oscar Winner).
- **Interviews**: Valuetainment (Patrick Bet-David), The School of Greatness.
- **Cross-industry Decisions**: Transitioning to venture capital, learning business from Steve Jobs.
